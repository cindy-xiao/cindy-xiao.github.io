Music

Columbia Pops | 2016-2018

Pops is an entirely student-run orchestra with biannual concerts and various performances during the school year. The music performed includes but is not limited to film and television scores, Broadway hits, and original student work.

In May 2018, we performed at Lincoln Center as part of a collaboration with Peking University in celebration of their 120th anniversary.

In addition to performing as part of the orchestra, I also arranged and performed music as part of a chamber group within Pops.

Precollege Division - Violin

Studied with Albert Markov. Played in the MSM Precollege Philharmonic Orchestra conducted by Jonathan Strasser.

NafME All-Eastern Orchestra | 2013

Students first audition to get into their regional orchestra, then their State orchestra, and then are eligible to audition for the All-Eastern Orchestra. States included in the eastern division are CT, DE, DC, EU, ME, MD, MA, NH, NJ, NY, PA, RI, and VT.
